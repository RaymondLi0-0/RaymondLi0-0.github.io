Rating: 9/10

Review:

Prospect Park Botanical garden will always hold a special place in my heart because of the numurous times that my elementary and middle school took my class to visit this place. First impressions was that the place had a lot of rediculously big tress and I would say as thick as 4 telephone booths combined toghether and as 5 school buses stacked on top of each other. There were also little ducks and fish I was able to see which was asthetically pleasing to watch. Despite the cold weather that I went in to the place, it was suprisingly still pcked with people. The fee to getting into Garden was at a reasonable price 5 dollarsfor each person and I was able to see a lot types of plants and experienced the very humid temperature when I was inside the greenhouse. Even outside when I was visiting the rock parks was I able to spend time out in nature, walking on the soft soil and rocky grounds. Although there weren't any flowers and or any leaves growig on the trees when I was at the garden, it was overall still not bad of a place to chill and appreciate the fresh air and the flowers and different cactuses, and trees that the place offered.
